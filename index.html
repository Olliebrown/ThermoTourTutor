About This Site

For help navigating this site, visit the Site Navigation and Tips page

In Spring of 2022, UW Stout professors Oai Ha, Keif Oss, and Seth Berrier started a research project to explore how a virtual tour of the campus central heating plant could help enhance learning outcomes in an undergraduate course in thermodynamics. This site is the ongoing work of that project and will provide a way to explore the central heating plan on the Stout campus and to learn about and assess basic thermodynamics concepts demonstrated in the plant.

This work is funded by a grant from the Xcel Energy Foundation
